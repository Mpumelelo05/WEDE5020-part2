About Us

The Ubuntu Youth Club is a non-profit organisation created to bridge the digital skills gap among young people. Many youth struggle when entering the workplace due to lack of technical skills. Our programme introduces digital literacy, technical training, and industry-relevant skills to prepare students for modern careers.

We offer accredited undergraduate programmes including higher certificates, diplomas, and degrees. Whether you are interested in business, information technology, or further studies, we provide the support needed to succeed academically and professionally.

Mission

To empower young people in South Africa by providing access to digital skills training, education, and mentorship opportunities that promote personal growth and employability.

Vision

To create a digitally empowered generation of young people who are confident, skilled, and ready to thrive in a rapidly evolving technological world.

Our organisation reflects the importance of digital education in today’s world. We provide young people with the skills needed for employment, innovation, and future growth.

Our programmes are delivered by experienced professionals who mentor students, ensuring they gain both technical knowledge and confidence.
